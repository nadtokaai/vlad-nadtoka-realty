Helping Investors Find Properties That Actually Perform

I'm Vlad Nadtoka — an Austin-based real estate agent dedicated to one thing: helping rental property investors find deals that actually cash-flow.

In a market full of glossy listings and optimistic projections, it's hard to know what's real. That's where I come in. I dig into the numbers, understand the local dynamics, and bring you opportunities that make sense for your portfolio — not just what's trending.

Whether you're a first-time investor or managing a growing portfolio, my approach is the same: investor-first, data-driven, no fluff. I believe you deserve to make decisions with confidence, not hope.

When I'm not analyzing deals, I'm tracking Austin's market trends, connecting with local property managers, and staying ahead of the data that matters to investors like you.
